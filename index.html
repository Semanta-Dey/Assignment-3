How do I place an order?

Simply select your desired fruits and vegetables, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout for payment and delivery options.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 24 to 48 hours depending on your location.

Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery is free for orders above $50. For orders below that amount, a small delivery fee applies.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) in select locations.
